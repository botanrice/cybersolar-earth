What is CyberSolar?
✕

CyberSolar; Grass is Greener is a music & web-rooted multi-modal art project.

It explores the fringes of climate psychology, poetic tech, stoicism, and subversive community building.

Navigate the desktop icons to enter each channel. The site evolves daily.
